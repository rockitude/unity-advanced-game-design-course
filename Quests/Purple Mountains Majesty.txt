Description

Most games in Unity simulate a world environment, which means they need terrain for characters to traverse. 

Watch this video to review how to create terrain for your game:

[Create Terrain](www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ep-uo15gOJIcom)

Create a new terrain object for your game and create a height map that includes hills, plateaus, mountains and sunken areas. Try to make something realistic that a player will be able to walk around on.
