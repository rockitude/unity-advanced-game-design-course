Description

Games are interactive by nature, and in Unity we add interactivity to our games using scripts that alter the properties of our objects: we can change their position in space, activate or deactivate them, add lights and colliders and all kinds of other stuff.

Watch this video to learn more about how to use scripting to alter properties of objects in Unity:

[Altering properties (light.intensity, etc.)](unity3d.com/learn/tutorials/modules/intermediate/scripting/properties)

Write a script for your game that accesses a property of a component to change its value.
