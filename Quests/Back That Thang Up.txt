**Description**

You've created your first Blender project, so now is a great time to remind you that you should always, **always**, **ALWAYS** save backup copies of your project. You can never predict when something weird will happen with your computer, and it's never a bad idea to have another copy of your files, should the originals mysteriously disappear. 

Watch this video to learn about saving and restoring projects:

[Saving, Restoring, Backups and Iterations](www.youtube.com/watch?v=DqCrAQehzQw&list=PL006307E237BB7FF6&index=4)
