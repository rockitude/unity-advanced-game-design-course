**Description**

Now that you can create primitive objects, it's time to get a little more complex. In this quest, you'll learn how to switch into edit mode and apply some basic alterations to the simple shapes you've created.

Check out this video to learn about editing objects, extruding and applying loop cuts:

[Edit Mode, Extrude and Loop Cuts](www.youtube.com/watch?v=OszPcbecX50&list=PL006307E237BB7FF6&index=5)

Add a primitive and make it into a dinosaur using extrude and loop cuts. Attach a screenshot of your dinosaur to your submission.
