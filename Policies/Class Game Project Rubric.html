This rubric shows what you'll be graded on each week as you complete your projects. Projects are due at a whole-class meeting each Monday or Tuesday, and new projects will be assigned after existing ones are handed in.

Category

Proficient

Needs Improvement

Unsatisfactory

Content

Projects completed by the student include all relevant requirements and exhibit thoughtfulness and creativity.

Projects are incomplete or lacking in creativity and effort.

Projects are incomplete or missing.

Deadlines

Projects are submitted in their completed form by the required time.

Projects are within one day of completion at the time they’re due.

Projects are not close to complete at the time they’re due.

Participation

Student was a willing participant in class and created assets as directed.

Student was an unwilling or grudging participant in class.

Student did not complete asset creation assignments as directed.
