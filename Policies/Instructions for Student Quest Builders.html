Instructions for Student Quest Builders

  • Quest building privileges unlock at 3000 XP (at the rank of CEO).
  • Quests built by students should be available only for students with 3000 XP until vetted and approved.
  • Include a summary of your quest requirements in the Submission section so they are visible while the quest is being evaluated.
  • Use a consistent icon for your entire quest chain.

Standard XP Values

15: Takes about 10 minutes, relatively simple or straightforward
25: Takes about 20 minutes, standard difficulty
50: Takes 30+ and/or considerable difficulty
100: Long term project

All values +/- 5 depending on specific circumstances

Suggested Quest Topics

  • Demonstrate how you did something cool
  • Show a new or different way of accomplishing a task
  • Provide an interesting opportunity for game analysis
